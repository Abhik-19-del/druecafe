Our Story

A corner of warmth
in a bustling city

Drue Cafe was born from a simple obsession — a belief that a great cup of coffee and honest food can make an ordinary day extraordinary. Tucked in the heart of Dhaka, we've been crafting moments of pause since 2019.

Every bean is sourced with care, every recipe is tested with love, and every guest is treated like family. This is more than a cafe — it's a ritual.
